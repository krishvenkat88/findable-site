The problem

The recommendation is where
the sale is decided.

"Best protein powder for runners who hate thick shakes"

A competitor was recommended. The right product existed but AI could not connect it to the query.

"Best anti-ageing serum for someone in their 50s"

The brand was mentioned. The right range within the brand was not. The customer had no reason to choose.

"Where to buy a luxury watch as a gift"

Grey market dealers were listed first. The authorised retailer with hundreds of stores was not mentioned.

"Best supplement for building muscle, not losing weight"

The brand had a product for exactly this. AI recommended a competitor because the product data did not carry the signal needed.

This is not an SEO problem. It is a product data structure problem. AI cannot make a confident recommendation from content written for human browsers.
